Welcome to our charity finder website!

To get started:
1. Find what category of charity you would like to donate to
2. Either use the Top Charities tab or use the Discover tab
3. If Top Charities used: Search through the top 10 charities
then click and explore to find the one that fits your needs
4. If Discover Charities used: Click the category button that matches what you
want and then look through the listed Charites and see what you like
5. Finally Click the link on the Charity and start donating

About Us

Our Charity Finder is a search engine similar to google, but specifically for charities!
As the internet is complex, we aim to provide all the resources necessary available
for our users, allowing them to find the charity they want to donate to.
